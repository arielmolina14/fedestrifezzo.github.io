As a Director, I worked on television documentaries such as C.A.L, el Congreso en Dictadura (2016), Viaje a la Patagonia Austral (2017) and La Victoria de Suipacha (2018). In all these cases, in addition to directing, I was also responsible for writing the scripts.

In 2021 I released Nosotras también estuvimos (We Were There Too), my first feature film. It is a personal project that I directed and produced with public and private funding. After being selected for various film festivals around the world, it was commercially released in theaters across the country. It was declared of cultural interest by the Buenos Aires City Legislature and by the National Senate. For this work, I was awarded the 2021 Lola Mora Prize for "contributing to the dissemination of images and coverage with a gender perspective, promoting equal rights and opportunities”. You can watch it on CINE.AR.

Between 2017 and 2023, I directed the program Con Voz y Voto, broadcast on YouTube, which won the Special Prize + Citizen Participation 2022 and 2023 and the Grand Legislative Prize 2023 awarded by the TAL (Latin American Open Television) network.
