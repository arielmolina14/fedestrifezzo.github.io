Photography Portfolio by Federico Strifezzo

Photography is essential in any audiovisual work. Well-used framing, color, lighting, and shot values allow you to generate the desired emotions and express ideas in a harmonious and aesthetic way.

Although I apply these ideas in all my work, I was Director of Photography on the documentaries REMAR (2025) and T-Shirt Collectors (2024), and on the short film The Last Newspaper Boy (2020), which won the Cine.ar award at the ConstruirCine festival.
