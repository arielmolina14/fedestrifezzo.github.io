Filmmaker Portfolio by Federico Strifezzo

As a Filmmaker, I create content for brands, companies, artists, and professionals. This can include reels for social media, fashion editorials, music videos, event coverage, weddings, reports, institutional videos, micro-documentaries, and branded content, among others.

In all cases, I am responsible for developing the idea, filming, adding music, creating effects and graphics, and editing.

I have worked for Anselmo Hotel (Curio Collection by Hilton), Zona Verde, Emergente Bar, Avia Terai, Vino Joven, Montessori Sunrise, Funes Boxing Club, I Love Runn, C.C.Richards, Ventus Motors, Antropotécnica, De Campo, Urbaser, ORGÍA theater, Tecnópolis, the Central Bank of Argentina, and the National Senate.
