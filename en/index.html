Federico Strifezzo

As a Filmmaker and Editor, I have worked on my own projects and those of others in film, television, advertising, and social media. Throughout my career, I have worked in key areas such as directing, screenwriting, photography, and editing. I have a Master's degree in documentary journalism and solid theoretical knowledge of audiovisual language.

I currently specialize in the convergence between traditional cinematic narrative and generative artificial intelligence, having evolved toward the exploration and professional application of AI tools in all stages of audiovisual production.

I work on both fully AI-generated productions and hybrid projects that integrate traditional techniques with emerging technologies, ranging from experimental narrative projects to advertising campaigns and social media content.
